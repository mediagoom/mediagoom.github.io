How it works

{{ page.description }}
MediaGoom will serve your video over the web. With MediaGoom you can power your webtv solution either for the internet or for your intranet.
You can use MediaGoom for your:
  • communication
  • e-learning
  • advertising

MediaGoom allows you to process your media file in order to be able to stream them on the web.
  • It uses the latest and most efficient protocols for accessing media over HTTP.
  • It uses HLS and mpeg dash to adaptively serve your media.
    • This means that your viewers will get a better experience when they have more network bandwidth available and less quality of viewing whit less bandwidth but will still be able to watch.
  • It is able to receive your file in upload and then encode your files using a mpeg dash encoder and an hls encoder.
  • It is able to playback your files on any browser on any device using a dedicated player.

MediaGoom is composed by several services divided for each task in order to give the most flexible solution.
MediaGoom node-play project allows you to access a ready to use solution witch combine most of the functionality.
You can use the oder project to build your on solution to fit your needs when you need more flexibility. With the oder MediaGoom projects you are able to:
  • drive your media workflow
  • package your media in HLS and MPEG-DAS
  • play your media in any browser and device

Getting Started

The simplest way to get started is to install node-play. In order to install node-play please follow the instruction you can find here.
After installing node-play you will able to upload your video files.
node-play will then take care of running a media workflow to
  • encode your files in different bitrate to empower adaptive streaming
  • package your files in mpeg dash and hls
  • Have a web page ready to allow your users to watch your media
